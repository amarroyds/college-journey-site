Deadline Calendar Template

Important — this is a template, not a fixed calendar. Exact dates change every year. Typical dates are shown (e.g., "Nov 1" for Early Action), but you must verify each one on the official site each cycle and record the confirmed date in your Google Sheet. Copy this whole timeline into a "Deadlines" tab in your Sheet at the start of your cycle, verify each date, and add the specific colleges on your list.
